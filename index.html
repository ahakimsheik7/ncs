COVID

In light of the increase of positive COVID-19 cases at NCS, please attend to the following: All New Century School employees are required to receive a COVID-19 vaccine, unless a reasonable accommodation is requested and approved. Moreover, employees are required to stay up to date on routine vaccinations to prevent illness; staying up to date with COVID-19 vaccinations is the leading public health strategy to prevent severe disease. Key times to get tested: -Those with symptoms should test immediately. -Staff exposed to COVID-19 and who do not have symptoms, should wait at least 5 full days after exposure before testing. Testing too early is likely to get an inaccurate result. -If you test positive, please apprise me and your immediate supervisor immediately. As well please apprise Deema; she will give you HR guidance. Last, indoor masking is recommended for all New Century School teachers, staff, students, and visitors, regardless of vaccination status. Mask up!
